Who Is Vanessa?

Vanessa is the youth arm of the TAC (and maybe the youth leg as well). Our main goal is to make this generation of young drivers the safest. No one should be lost to the road and we believe that this generation is the one to make 'zero' a reality – it’s not as impossible as it sounds.

We’re such regulars at major music festivals, open days and community events that we’ve become an essential part of the Victorian calendar. Offering free breath-testing, safe driving tips, drug and alcohol info and general good vibes.

Where Does She Go?

If it’s going off, we’re there. You’ll find us at everything from one-day inner-city festivals to university open days in Warrnambool and then getting our dance on at a multi-day camping festival in Lorne.

We work closely with organisers to make sure that once the event is over and the dust has settled, everyone has had a good time and makes it home safely.
