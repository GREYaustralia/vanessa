The Mothership

If you’re looking for a big bus with a sweet roof deck that’s covered in Oslo Davis drawings, look no further than Big V, the largest of our fleet and festival frequenter.

One of Big V’s main drawcards is its awesome photo booth, where festival goers can get their snap immortalised on a free key ring.

Getting around to all of the larger events on the calendar, Big V offers:
- Free breath testing
- A chill out zone for people to relax in the shade
- Entertainment and messaging shown on inbuilt screens
- Fun games that incorporate road safety messaging such as jenga, beer goggles, hopscotch and the famous spinning wheel
- Free merchandise and prizes like ponchos and sunnies
- Rooftop viewing deck

Vehicle Specs:
When set up: 12.5m L x 2.5m W x 3.8 H
When packed down: 14.5m L x 7m W x 5.5m H
Weight: 15.7 tonne
Power requirements: 3 x 15 amp
On board generator, but plug-in power preferred

The Muscle

Bute is the heavy duty one in the fleet, making it perfect for getting to all those hard to reach places. You’ll find Bute at the snow, Open Days and anywhere else that needs decent traction to get to.

This 5-Star safety rated Ford Ranger Ute is also great at supporting Big V at larger events, supplying an extra water and breath testing station.

Bute includes:
- Free breath testing
- A chill out zone for people to relax in the shade
- Fun games that incorporate road safety messaging such as jenga, beer goggles, hopscotch and the famous spinning wheel
- Free merchandise and prizes like ponchos and sunnies

Footprint when set up: 7mL x 4.5mW x 1.9mH
Not set up: 5.4mL x 1.9mW x 1.9mH

The Minivan

Mini V is what you get when you put Big V through the dryer. Being small, Mini V can make it to all those festivals and Open Days that Big V’s trunk is just too large for. This 5-star rated Mercedes Vito is also great at supporting Big V or just standing alone like Rocky celebrating at the top of those steps.

Mini V’s got:
